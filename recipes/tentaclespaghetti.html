(Somebody Toucha My) Tentacle Spaghett

Description

Just a big 'ol bowl of tentacles, like your daddy used to make it.

Throw in some eyeballs for a little extra pizzazz.

Ingredients

  • Tentacles
  • More Tentacles
  • Bowl
  • Eyeballs (if you're blessed)

Steps

  1. Grasp Tentacles (firmly, if you're smart)
  2. Lift Tentacles (careful, now)
  3. Cry
  4. Sprinkle tentacles into bowl at a high rate of speed. Don't get any tears on them.
  5. Consider what you've done - are there enough tentacles?
  6. There aren't enough tentacles- add more
  7. Eyeball time!
  8. Spin bowl and let the chanting commence
