Fried Unicorn Tears

Description

Exclusive to the elite, this dish is a delicacy to be reckoned with. The tears of a unicorn, pan-fried to perfection. Serve with a sprinkle of gold dust for added opulence.

Ingredients

  • Unicorn Tears
  • Gold Dust (optional, but recommended)
  • Frying pan

Steps

  1. Find a unicorn (be sure to have your fairy dust handy)
  2. Gently coax a tear from the unicorn's eye
  3. Collect the tear in a small container
  4. Heat frying pan on medium-high heat
  5. Carefully pour in tear
  6. Cook for approximately 5 seconds (cooking time will vary depending on the size of the tear)
  7. Remove from heat, dust with gold dust and serve.
